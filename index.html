Project Idea

Our idea for this webpage is going to be an ordering system of a sub sandwich restaurant we visited a very well known chain restaurant that prvides such sandwiches and that is how we got inspired, in this system the users select how would they like thier sandwiches made in details, by choosing from a variety of bread types,vegetables, sauces, and others they can also add any kind of snack they want and what type of drink they are going to have. Finally, they get to choose their payment method and how they want the get the order, by pick-up or delivery.
